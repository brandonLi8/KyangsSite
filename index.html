About Me

Hi! I’m Kevin Yang, a Fairview High school student and research intern at CU Boulder. I am passionate about scientific research, specifically at the intersection of materials science and energy innovation. I have worked on designing a light-converting greenhouse film material for crop production and a novel radiative cooling bilayer paint for passive home cooling. I have presented research and won awards at various symposiums and competitions at the national, state, and local level. Outside of research and school, I am a fan of video games and watching Youtube. Currently, I am working on publishing a paper on my greenhouse film research and have submitted the manuscript to Nature.
